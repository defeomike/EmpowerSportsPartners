About

ESP is a GLOBAL investment & development company that EMPOWERS entrepreneurs to improve lives through HEALTH, FITNESS & SPORTS.

Through partnership & growth capital, ESP makes long-term investments in portfolio companies in addition to providing geographic & strategic advice. Our approach is to offer a supportive & non-intrusive platform for the entrepreneur to maintain control, expand his/her operations & execute a mutually shared vision.
